Hi! I'm Cyrus Hung

Student, Game Developer, Programmer, Web Developer, MUN Delegate, Gamer, Cook

About me

Born in Hong Kong, I'm a current high school senior living in Shanghai - a city I've come to call home in the last 14 years. Being able to return to my hometown every new years for Hong Bao while being able to enjoy Boba tea in Shanghai, I'm able to live a life that's the best of both worlds.

I currently attend Concordia International School Shanghai, where I've made some of my best friends and met amazing teachers who have inspired and impacted me for life. Being able to attend an international school also means I get to collaborate with a wide range of students, and be part of a community of many cultures.

My foremost passion is Computer Science. Since young, I've been fascinated with computers and software and have been programming for years. I intend to pursue computer science in college.

Interests

Here's some things I like to do in my spare time!

Game Development

I began developing games in middle school, starting with Scratch and later moving on to Unity. I'm currently the leader of my school's GameDev club, where I teach other students and lead projects, check them out below!

Programmer

I love programming in my free time, whether it's translating concepts I learn such as gauss-jordan elimination into code, creating a coach assistant app, or coding fun projects like making Conway's Game of Life using Java and Swing.

Web Developer

I also enjoy creating websites in my free time. I'm currently the leader of my school's WebDev club, the web developer for influxanalytics.org, and also created SpeechPacer, a website for helping children who stutter.

Caring for pets

Living in a house with 4 cats and a dog, all of whom used to be strays, I find that caring for them is the best way for me to relieve stress.
I'm very allergic to animal dander, but I refuse to let that stop me from petting them as much as I can.

Model United Nations

An MUN delegate since 7th grade, I enjoy researching and debating about world issues. I was recently awarded Best Delegate at my last conference and am the current President of General Assembly for CISSMUN, a 700+ international conference.

Baking

I've been cooking and baking desserts with my dad since elementary school. Even after he left for work I enjoy making food in my spare time. Notable desserts include are macarons, creme brulee, and a flaming alaska. I actually don't have a sweet tooth - what I love is the process.

An implementation of Conway's Game of Life I made through Java and the Swing UI interface for familiarizing myself with cellular automata and UI desigin. Allows the user to draw their own patterns and seeds, set grid size and time step duration.
